Our Services

Climate Naturals was set up to create an ecosystem for a farm to fork experience in spices. It is our stated mission to make available the best grown spices to the lovers of good food across the world. Our services often go beyond the simple sourcing and distribution functions and our expertise is often lent to other similar businesses and large B2B operations.

Providing organic, conventional, and pesticide-free options for the food and beverage industry.

The B2B space comes with its own set of challenges in quality, customer tastes and delivery schedules. Multinational HORECAs and supermarkets, including Kerala's own Supplyco, have enjoyed our impeccable service, from packages as small as 10 gms to bulk consignments. Our commitment to agreements, no matter how stringent, has made us a go-to supplier for all spice requirements.

Retail trade of spices sourced from the finest estates

Cardamom, Clove, Pepper, Nutmeg and Mace are our current portfolio of products, and the end-to-end presence of Climate Naturals in the entire supply chain of spices is a defining characteristic of our business. Be it 10-gram sachets or bulk wholesale requirements, we have you covered. Excellent home-grown capabilities in packaging, labelling and transporting gives us that extra edge over competition.

Scientific sourcing, for best quality and price

TSpices need special climates to thrive, and sourcing them from the right estates is key to its quality. At Climate Naturals we support farmers right from the beginning of each season, ensuring the best of the harvest reach us, and using proven scientific methods they are graded, sorted and packed to retain utmost freshness as it reaches the end consumer

Production, preparation, process - 3P model implementation

When every part of the supply chain is transparent and traceable, accountability automatically sets-in. This stringent process sets us apart from any other supplier, with accurate data helping us learn and improve our systems all the time. Also, we extend this capability to all those who wish to source and trade in spices.
