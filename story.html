Our Story

It was in 2012, from Kerala the home to the world's best spices, hit upon the idea of making spices available online for both retail and bulk purchasers. Especially since the spice markets and trade was an obscure phenomenon for most people, and very few had access to the end farmer or ways to ensure quality.

Climate Naturals comes from a family that has almost 200 years of experience in cultivating spices, on over 100 acres of land. A variety of spices have been successfully nurtured and commercially sold from these estates, the chief among them being Cardamom, followed by Pepper, Ginger, Nutmeg and Cloves.

The real advantage comes from sourcing directly from the farmers or from auctions, eliminating middlemen. When it comes to Cardamom, the Idukki District in Kerala provides almost 90% of the country's output.

In a world that was seeing more and more prevalence for the 'Direct-To-Consumer' model, the spices market was till shying away from this method. Creating an alternate pathway for consumers, be it a home buyer, a retail buyer, a wholesaler or exporter, towards honestly procured spices, from the best plantations was the need of the hour.

Thus born was Climate Naturals, perhaps the world's first online spice ordering platform, also available as an App, if you're the truly mobile kind.

Today Climate Naturals clocks sales of 450 tonnes. Australia, Turkey, Oman, Austria and Iran import them, even as the company ventures into newer pastures in Europe and the Middle East. In the domestic market CN supplies to leading food and spices' brands.

From truly humble beginning and a lot of learnings through 13 years, Climate Naturals is now poised to transform into a 1000 crore entity, with the launch of our own brands in the retail market.
